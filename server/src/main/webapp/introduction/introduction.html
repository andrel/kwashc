KWASHC

The Zombies have overrun the world, reaping havoc and killing most of society.

Internet is the only secure way of communication, but Zombie crackers are performing automated attacks and creating chaos on communication sites.

An elite group of heros called the League of Zombie Hacker Heros are fighting back.

To become member of this elite unit, you must prove worthy by competing in the Super Hero Challenge.

The Heros Challenge simulates an environment where you are the defenders.

Your challenge is to defend a simple blog application from the simulated onslaught of Zombie Crackers. Our attack server will both attack and assist you to secure your site.

The first hero or team of heros to complete all of the 14 tests will be welcomed into the League of Zombie Hacker Heros.
